What Makes a Good Freelance Notion Template?

Before we dive in, here's what separates useful templates from beautiful-but-useless ones:

  • Pre-populated sample data — you should see immediately how it works
  • Database relationships — not just flat lists, but connected information
  • Multiple views — kanban for projects, calendar for deadlines, table for invoices
  • Actionable instructions — tells you when to use each section, not just what it is

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a paid Notion plan?

No. All these templates work with the free Notion plan. A paid plan (€10/month) adds unlimited blocks and file uploads, which helps if you're storing documents, but it's not required to start.

How long does setup take?

For each template: 5-15 minutes. You click "Duplicate", the template copies to your workspace, you delete the sample data and add your own. Most people are fully set up within an hour.

Can I customize these templates?

Yes, completely. Notion templates are starting points. Add properties, change views, rename sections — make them yours. The sample data is there to show you how the system works.
